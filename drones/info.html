What is a drone?

An unmanned aerial vehicle, also known as a drone, is a flying vehicle without an onboard human pilot.


Uses of drones

Drones are used in many different ways, including:

Military

Drones are often used in the military for training, but also are used for combat for situations that would be dangerous for soldiers, and for other duties such as delivering supplies.

Commercially

Drones are also used commercially. They are often used for filming high shots in the TV and movie industry. Some companies have also looked into using drones as delivery systems.

As a hobby

Many people also like to use drones as a hobby to play with.
